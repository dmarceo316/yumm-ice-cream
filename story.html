The Story

A Yummy Story!

The Yummm company came to fruition in 2018 when 2 inspired individuals Julie and Adam moved to the city of Seattle, Washington originally for work in the respected fields, but found a deep love and appreciation for ice cream. Julie started creating her own variations of scoops using locally sourced ingredients from neighboring communities such as the Snohomish, Yakima, and Kitsap counties. Using milk from carefully selected cattle along with fresh produce, the Yumm co creates a litany of unique and seasonal flavors that you can only find in the Northwest. The flagship store was established in mid 2018 in Capitol Hill, and has now acquired locations around Seattle, and has even made its way to neighboring city Portland, Oregon!
